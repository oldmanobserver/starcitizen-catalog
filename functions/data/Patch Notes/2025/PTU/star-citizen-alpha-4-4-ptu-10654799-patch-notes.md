# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**New Event Mission: Vanduul-Tech Smugglers**
Vanduul-Tech Smugglers is a PvE Mission centered around gaining access to high value loot rooms inside a space station. The mission will see players investigating the Shattered Blade base in upon arriving the player will see Vanduul attacking the Shattered Blade. Players will be asked to come for a reward and end up ambushed the player can then explore and find encrypted data and upload it to complete the mission Brace for impact as players dive into the newly unlocked Nyx System, where a familiar menace—the Vanduul—makes their chilling debut in the Persistent Universe. What begins as an investigation mission quickly spirals into a web of deception.

Players ally with a seemingly oppressed faction under Vanduul attack, only to uncover their true identity: the Shattered Blade. After a promised reward turns into a deadly ambush, players must hunt down encrypted data across hostile zones—Space Station, Asteroid Base, and a ‘Traversable’ Polaris ship.

Each location reveals brutal skirmishes between the Shattered Blade and the Vanduul, exposing a dark truth: the Shattered Blade has been trafficking alien tech and lifeforms. As the ASD storyline unfolds, players uncover secrets that could reshape the balance of power in the 'verse.

> **Weapons and Items**
**New FPS Weapon: Triple Threat**
Firing three shotgun shells with each pull of the trigger thanks to it's triple-barrel construction, the bolt-action Negotiator pistol is ready to blast its way through anything from the smallest disagreements to massive dustups.

Triple Threat Stats;
- Weight: Light
- Damage: 2 tap medium armour to the chest within 10m (PVP)
- Harsh damage fall off
- 9 pellets, reasonable spread
- Projectile Velocity: 550
- Aim down Sight Time: 0.21
- RPM: 60
- Mag size:  4(12)
- Fires 3 barrels at once
Design Notes from Zac:
"I adore this thing, a back up close range, bolt action, drum mag pistol to help you out in a pinch with a punishing mag size compensated with incredible fire power. It's the perfect pocket sized CQC weapon.
You can find this weapon along with the Killshot and the Pulveriser in the weapon shops in Levski
It is also the weapon featured in 'Single Weapon Elimination' in AC for the PTU.
