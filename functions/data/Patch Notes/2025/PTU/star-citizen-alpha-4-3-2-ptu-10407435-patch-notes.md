# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Salvage Refining and Economy Adjustments**
RMC and Construction Materials repriced. RMC sale prices reduced. Construction material refinery yields decreased while increasing sale prices.


**Additional Gameplay Updates**
- PVP Missions will now be in their own tab on the contract manager

> **Ships and Vehicles**
**Aegis Reclaimer Cargo Elevator Updates**
Added cargo grid to the lift. Fixed animation length. Fixed animation to make lift go further down. Moved interaction point to be reachable by the player when cargo is loaded on lift.

**Additional Updates**
- Nova gatling and Scorpius turret added to shops.

> **Core Tech**
- Onyx Facility OC performance optimizations
