# Testing/Feedback Focus
This initial build is a stability focused playtest for Evocati on EPTU. There may be features in various states of completeness but we are currently not focusing on feedback on these.
-     Stability & Bugfixes
-     Second Life Resource Drive (rewards are now functioning for the event)
Player’s will be tasked with different missions to achieve this including:
1. Resource Gathering - They ask the player to source and gather X resources.
1. Recover Cargo - Players go to retrieve cargo from a vessel that has broken down/attacked. Or retrieve cargo from a stolen stash.
1. Support Attacked Ship - Defend a cargo ship from waves of pirates trying to destroy and rob the ship.
1. Hauling - Retrieve resources that have been processed and are ready to be delivered.
