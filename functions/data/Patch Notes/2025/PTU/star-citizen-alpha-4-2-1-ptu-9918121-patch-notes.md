# Features & Gameplay
> Gameplay
**New Mission Type: Ship Escort**
In this new Mission Archetype, players will be tasked with fending off enemy ships whilst escorting friendly ships into a hangar around the asteroid mining bases as well as escorting them out until they can QT away. This mission type comes in 6 difficulty levels under the Foxwell group in both Stanton and Pyro and awards Scrip upon completion.
