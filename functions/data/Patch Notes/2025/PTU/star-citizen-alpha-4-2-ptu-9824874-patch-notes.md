# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
- Updated Radiation HUD to further reflect levels of severity

**Storm Breaker Polish**
Art, Audio Mixing, LOD, Lighting, Shuttle, and Performance Polish. Removed ability to set spawn points to Facility interior and moved them to multiple satellite locations outside the facility. Further tweaks and polish for lightning and rain effects, min / max length and min / max spawn frequency for lightning. Keep doors opened for the egg nursery room at the end of the release procedure. Added additional scientists to datalab in cafeteria and weapons testing room."


**Apex Valakkar Balance and Polish Pass**
Changed APEX Valakkar mouth to no longer provide bonus damage. (Weakspots are full damage while armored areas take vastly less damage)
Further added Apex Valakar and location Radiation effects. The Hazards that the Valakar create will have radiation that decays over time in strength. Apex Valakar Animation and skin texture polish.
- Reduced reinforcements call time from 40 to 30 seconds
- Disabled disorientation behavior to improve fight pacing
- Increased Adult valakkar projectile damage from 25 to 40
- Improved consistency of VLK adult accuracy
- Increased Juvinile valakkar projectile damage from 9 to 15
- Increased apex valakkar explosion max radius from 10 to 20
- Increased apex valakkar explosion damage from 2.5 to 4 (explosions hit multiple body parts)
- Increased apex valakkar projectile damage from 5 to 20
- Increased health of valakkar adult from 350 to 475
- Disabled being able to stun adult and juvenile Valakkars 

> **Locations**
- Adjusting rain volume density on Hurston and Arccorp
- Enabled Dynamic Rain on Pyro IV
> **Ships and Vehicles**
**Missile and Torpedo Balance Updates**
Across the board, missiles and torpedoes have been readjusted to increase minimum lock ranges and lock timers, added a minimum activation distance for torpedoes (~1500m), decreased min/max explosion radius on hit, and increased health.
> **Weapons and Items**
- VOLT Shotgun Audio Polish
> **Core Tech**
- Removed ability to disable clouds completely from the user.cfg
- Extended puddle draw distances
- 3d Comms Notification Polish
