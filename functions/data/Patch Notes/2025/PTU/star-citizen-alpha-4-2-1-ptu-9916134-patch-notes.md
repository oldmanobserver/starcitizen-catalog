# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
- Further Wikelo Recipe Updates

**Station Defense Updates**
Increased health and damage output of size 10 station defenses. Made improvements to the way they take damage to ensure the weapon never stops firing before the base is destroyed and will now delete the weapons on destruction.

To prevent uninvited players getting aboard docked ships (technically any ship near enough a station) we now teleport trespassing players out of ships they don't own (unless in party with owner) when within 5km of an ATC with teleport points.


> **Ships and Vehicles**
**Ship Flight Tuning Changes**
► Heavy Fighters & Interceptors:
Minor rebalance and a general increase in maximum angular speed, providing improved maneuverability across all ships in these categories.

► Medium Fighters & Interceptors:
A comprehensive tuning pass has been applied to Medium Fighters to deliver more distinct flight characteristics per ship.
Alien ships such as the Glaive, Scythe, San'tok.yāi, and Reliant variants are included.
Key adjustments focus on:
- Angular rotation max speeds
- Angular accelerations
- Linear acceleration (Gs)
- SCM speeds

► Gun Boats and other medium-size ships:
Several ships in this class have received updates aimed at refining flight dynamics, including:,
- Angular rotation speed
- Linear acceleration (Gs)
- SCM and NAV top speeds

► Other aspects of some of the ships in all the categories mentioned have been addressed:
Mercury Star Runner:
- Increased Quantum Fuel capacity for extended travel range
- Slight buff to critical health hardpoints
- Retains previously boosted top speeds
Ares Inferno:
- SF7B Gatling spread got reduced for improved weapon accuracy.
400i: 
- Added an additional S2 Powerplant and Cooler back to the default loadout

**Other Ship Updates**
- Salvage Balance: Reduced Reclaimer Hull Scraping Efficiency and increased Vulture and Fortune Efficiency
