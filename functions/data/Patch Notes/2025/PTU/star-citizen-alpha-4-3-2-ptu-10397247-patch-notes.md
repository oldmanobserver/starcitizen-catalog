# Features & Gameplay
> **Weapons and Items**
- Updated the following weapons to have consistent SCU sizes (these were inconsistent outliers): Volt LMG, FS9, P4AR, P6LR, F55, Arclight, Ravager

**Heavy weapons (Shotguns, Snipers, LMGs)**
New sway profiles on heavy weapons, Sniper has it's own profile (except the aztkav)

**Snipers**
- Fixed an issue where ballistic snipers would not penetrate shields
**Atzkav**
- Increased damage from 100 to 120 (can 1 tap heavy heads)
- Increased projectile speed from 500 to 750
- Fixed all atzkav optic skins to have a functional optic
**Scalpel**
- Aim down sight time decreased from 0.4 to 0.28
**A03**
- Aim down sight time decreased from 0.435 to 0.35

**LMGs**
VOLT LMG
- Damage increased from 6.5 - 9
- Min fire rate with heat modifier increased from 0.15 to 0.2
- Damage modifier with heat decreased from 8 - 6
- This should make the VOLT LMG more effective at low heat keeping it's damaging profile feeling good throughout usage as well as buffing the high end


**SMGs**
**Ripper**
- Fall off damage start increased from 22.5m to 35m (Has no fall off akin to other laser weapons)
**Custodian**
- Decreased max spread from 7.25 to 4.5
- decreased shot per spread from 1.02 to 0.77
- increased damage per shot from 12 to 13
- Aim down sight time decreased from 0.235 to 0.21
**C54**
- Decreased max spread from 6.5 to 5
- Decreased spread per shot from 1.35 to 0.8
**Quartz**
- heat damage multiplier adjusted from 3 - 1
- Heat, Heat multiplier adjusted from 1.5 - 1
- Damage increased from 185 to 225

**LMGs**
**F55**
- Decreased max spread from 13.5 to 6.5
- Increased spread decay from 2.5 to 4

**Special**
Reverted an unintended adjustment to the animus missile launcher (speed and range decreased reverted)
