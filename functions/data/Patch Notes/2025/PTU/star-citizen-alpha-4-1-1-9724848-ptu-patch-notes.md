Alpha Patch 4.1.1 has been released to all backers to test!   Patch should now show: VERSION 4.1.1-PTU.9724848.

# Features & Gameplay

- **Locations**
Fleet Week Hall is now Open!  Located at the Bevic Convention Center at Area18, the halls are now open for testing and looking around.  In addition, we have the Aegis Idris Experience.  An Idris is docked at the top of the ArcCorp tower at Area18, ready to be explored as part of the celebration.  

- **Gameplay**
- Hunt the Polaris can now be joined by anyone, anywhere, as a cooperative mission

- **Ships and Vehicles**
- Further decreases to angular speed capacities for the Polaris and 890j
- Increased max range of Polaris S6 turret guns
