# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Alliance Aid and Industrial Mission Polish**
Increased Hauling Mission max crate size and reduce overall needed amount of goods needed to haul. Fixing incorrect max rep for industrial salvage missions. Fixing Molina Mold is not spawning in the Quarantine Area.

**Added new Alliance Aid Milestone missions**
These special missions will be unlocked when players reach half the progress bar of each division. These missions will be playable only once and they are meant to boost the progress a little bit more.
- **Transport: **Pickup vent filters from Hurston and Mold Treatment from microTech to then deliver the commodities to Levski
- **Collection:** Find and deliver Molina Mold. These commodities are mushrooms spawning in Levski
- **Defense:** A ship in Nyx carrying two cryopods with some VIPs is under attack. Save the cargo and transport those VIPs to microTech

**Additional Gameplay Updates**
- Including new inventory for Pyro Rest-stop's Black Markets
> **Ships and Vehicles**

**Ground Vehicle Missile Balance**
Missiles used for ground vehicles have been split off into their own versions for proper balance. This will include changes to minimum and maximum ranges, lock times, and substantial increases to damage. 


**Additional Ship Updates**
- Further Aurora Mk I Gold Standard Art and Audio Polish
- Avenger Series Audio Polish
> **Core Tech**
- Star Map UI: Added ability to scroll text that exceeds the bounds of the container
