# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Rock Cracker Operation - Breaker Stations**
The Rock Cracker Dungeon is a sandbox Location Event based in Nyx that will utilize new Asteroid Mining Stations to task players with rebooting one of these derelict stations so they can mine a large mineral resource at the center of the asteroid. This will primarily be a PVE focused sandbox activities with players fighting NPCs and creatures that have infested the dungeon during the course of restoring the station’s mining capabilities. For feedback focus, this dungeon experience is designed with 4-6 players in mind.

These locations can have sandbox activities that include interior missions such as recover item, missing person, and eliminate specific and exterior missions to recover cargo, kill ship bounty, support attack ship, and ship wave attacks.

There are currently 60 Exclusive Rockcracker locations, 30 Contested Rockcracker locations, and 20 General Mission Rockcracker locations.
