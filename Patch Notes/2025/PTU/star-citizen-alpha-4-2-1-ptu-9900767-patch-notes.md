# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Wikelo Recipe Updates**
No more random rewards

All collector rewards will become deterministic, meaning you'll know what you are going to get when accepting a mission.
We've defined and split ingredients for the mission into three distinct categories.
►Core Content Items [Items that are guaranteed with specific content e.g. ]
- Example: Carinite with Align and Mine
►Rare Content Items [Items that have a very low spawn rate per run(Below X%), with specific content]
- Example: Pure Carinite for Align and Mine
►Content Items [Items that spawn associated with Specific content but are not guaranteed, if you look you will likely find ]
- Example: Ace Interceptor Helmets for Ship Battles

► Collector Rebalance
All the recipes have been re-done and re-balanced
2 new tabs have been added to the contracts manager to accommodate all the new recipes. Wikelo - Vehicles and Wikelo - Other Items.


**Greenzone Security Re-Activation**
Greenzone security around Stanton Space Stations has been re-activated in 4.2.1. This includes a complete re-balance of security forces with escalating response to accumulated crimerates in the area.


**Further Refining Improvements**
Cost Reduction for most goods in regards to Refining Cost. Removal of Discard Cost to Refining.


**Additional Gameplay Updates**
- Resource Gathering Mission, Bugfixing, and Corporation Branding Polish
- FPS Mining Beam Brightness has Been Slightly Reduced
- Added chance of new Small Pure Carinite FPS minable in the Hathor Site cave
- 2 Handed Carryables are now thrown up with the player takedowns instead of dropped
> **Ships and Vehicles**
- Executive Ship Component Loadouts have been reverted to previous higher-tier versions
- Greatly Reduced Incorrectly High Idris Turret Health
- Updated base Valkyrie to have the new Doors and Door Panels from the Asgard 
> **Weapons and Items**
- New FPS Weapon: VOLT Pistol  (Weapon lootable and in AC. Ammo added today into shops)
- The 5 Qualities of Valakkar Pearls now have separate names so players can identify the Quaility
