# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Onyx Facility and Mission Polish**
Reduced the Onyx Facility Phase 3 mission Plasma Fuel Cell requirements to 1. General Onyx Facility Performance, set dressing, and audio Polish.


**Medical Bed Respawn Updates**
Updated medbed respawn flow for separate medgel use per Tier injury. T3 costs 5 medgel, T2 costs 10 medgel, T1 costs 20 medgel. Players will only be charged once for the highest tier the player has, no matter how many injuries are healed at once.
Medical bed respawn flow updated with tiered allowed respawn ranges. Outside of these distances you will not be able to respawn at your ship.
- T1 - 750,000,000km
- T2 - 8,000km
- T3 - 50km
