# Star Citizen Alpha Patch 4.3.1
Alpha Patch 4.3.1 has been released to Evocati, and is now available to test!   Patch should now show: VERSION 4.3.1-PTU.10244489.
- Audience: All Backers
- Server Info: PTU Channel - US Only
- Long Term Persistence: Enabled 
- Mesh Configuration: 7:3:600
- Starting aUEC: 15,000,000
Note: This patch does not contain all of the intended features, which will be added iteratively to the notes as they become available. Additionally, some features may be implemented in a partial state and as such are not ready for testing and subject to removal.  When those features reach a playable state they will be detailed on the notes and added to the "Testing Focus".  Please issue council all issues related to those in testing focus and in general gameplay.

# Features & Gameplay
> **Gameplay**
**Onyx Facility and Mission Polish**
Further Onyx Facility Mission and Location Polish. Further Polish for Art, Environment, VFX, and Audio for Onyx Facility locations and mission flow. 

**Medbed and MedGel Updates**
We've made a few balance changes to the amounts of medgel contained in canisters and tiered medical beds.
- Canister changed from 100 to 200 medgel.
- T3 Bed changed from 100 (1 revive) capacity to 200 (2 revives)
- T2 Bed changed from 200 (2 revive) capacity to 400 (4 revives)
- T1 Bed changed from 400 (4 revive) capacity to 600 (6 revives)
> Weapons and Items
- Volt LMG: VOLT LMG VFX Polish. Added to Arena Commander
