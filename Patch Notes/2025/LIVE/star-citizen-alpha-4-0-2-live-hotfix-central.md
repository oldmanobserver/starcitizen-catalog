# 4.0.2 Supply Or Die Commodity Adjustments
3.24.2025 - Prices have been updated with supply and demand while Supply or Die is running.
This change will not remove the Cargo Hauling side of the event players are enjoying but will make sure they payment matches the effort given by the other routes players get these resources in.
 
These new supply and demand prices are not permanent and will be adjusted back after the event.

**Buy and Sell Prices for these items have been greatly increased.**
- Tin is increased by 7300% 
- Ice is increased to 3300% 
- Copper by 2900% 
- Corundum by 2900%
- Detatrine is 50% the price. From 9000 to 4500
- Supply and refresh rate for these have been drastically lowered for the event
- All of these changes, bring the Effect Value Appraisal for Shop Sourcing vs Mining to about Even.


# Using the HOTFIX channel
The HOTFIX channel will become available for periods of time when the client-side of the game needs the hotfix. This channel will point to the LIVE environment and using this will retain and add progress to your LIVE account as if you were playing normally.
The best way to play on HOTFIX is to rename your LIVE folder to HOTFIX and verify using the HOTFIX channel on the launcher. Reverse this process to go back to LIVE once it is released or you would like to stop using this channel.
